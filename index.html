About MARVELLOUS!


Hello everybody. I'm Marvellous, an aspiring software engineer currently studying Computer science. I liked computer games as a kid, and I got introduced to Unity game Engine in high school. I started with Unity, and I realized that I might have loved games, but I did not enjoy game development.

I noticed I enjoyed problem-solving with c#, the programming language I was using for Unity back then, and I focused on it. I looked for random questions and tried to solve them, When it was time to choose my career, I knew what I wanted, so I decided to study computer science.

In my first year, I was distracted by my grades, and I did not focus on programming. I grew tired of thinking about grades. During my first-year break, I stumbled upon back-end development. I loved it and started developing my programming skills in that particular direction.

I love building things from scratch and also trying out different technologies. I am interested in backend development, Microservices Architecture and Algorithm and Data Structures. When I am not coding, I relax by playing video games, watching movies, and I enjoy playing board games a lot.
